Community Quizz!

Groups

The Administrator has the ability to assign Users and Testers to groups.

Quizz

The Testers have the ability to create quizzes specific to each group they are apart of.

Users

The Users have the ability to take quizzes within groups they are apart of.

User Results

The Users can view their test scores immediately after taking the quizz.

Student Results

Testers can see the results of quizzes from each student.
